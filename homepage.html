About Investor Self-Service Portal

Welcome to the Investor Self-Service Portal, your one-stop solution for managing investments with ease and convenience. Our platform empowers you to:

  • Perform fresh purchases, additional investments, redemptions, and scheme switches online.
  • Access comprehensive account summaries and detailed transaction histories.
  • Update your personal profile and manage linked bank accounts effortlessly.
  • Stay informed with regular updates and personalized investment insights.

Our goal is to provide you with intuitive tools and reliable support to optimize your investment experience. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out, we're here to help you achieve your financial goals.
