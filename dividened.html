Why Change Your Dividend Option?

Choosing the right dividend option can impact your investment strategy. Reinvesting dividends allows your investment to grow faster over time, while receiving payouts provides immediate income.

Instructions:

  1. Enter your folio number.
  2. Select the scheme you want to modify.
  3. Choose either reinvest or payout for your new dividend option.
  4. Click "Submit Change" to update your preference.
